School Art Projects Collection

A showcase of my school art projects: ceramics, nail design, and a hairstyling poster.

Ceramic Lotus‑Pad Tile — Art Class

I created this ceramic lotus‑pad tile in my art class. It includes hand‑sculpted frogs and lotus arranged in a grid pattern. I painted and glazed each piece to give it a shiny, realistic finish.

Nail Art Designs — Hairstyling Class

These nail designs were created in my hairstyling class. I practiced applying gems, patterns, and color combinations to create clean, professional‑looking nail art samples.

Hairstyling Poster — Culminating Project

This poster was my culminating project in hairstyling class. I designed it to show creativity, color coordination, and different nail polish styles in a classroom display.
