Published Black-and-White Artwork

This black-and-white portrait painting was created during the 2024–2025 academic year. Its high contrast and detailed shading led to its selection for publication in the Thistletown Collegiate Institute school magazine.

The piece explores the depth of human emotion through monochrome tones, focusing on the play between light and shadow. Being featured in the school magazine was a significant milestone in my artistic journey.
